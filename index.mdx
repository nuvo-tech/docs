## Authentication

All requests authenticate with an API key issued from your Nuvo dashboard under **Settings → Integrations**. Webhooks additionally include an ECDSA signature you can verify against Nuvo's public key.

Need help? Reach out to your Nuvo contact or email support@nuvo.com.
